About me

Greetings, esteemed visitor. I am Krishna Chandra Acharya, a dedicated advocate for human rights and social justice. Rooted in a background as a social teacher, I have dedicated myself to championing the rights of the marginalized, particularly children and women.

My journey into the realm of social activism began with a deep-seated belief in the transformative power of education. As a social teacher, I witnessed firsthand the profound impact that education can have on individuals and communities, empowering them to break free from the shackles of poverty and oppression. This experience ignited within me a passion for social justice and a commitment to fighting for the rights of the most vulnerable members of society.

In my role as the Chairman of Fowep Nepal , I have the honor and privilege of leading initiatives aimed at breaking the cycle of poverty and inequality in impoverished villages across Nepal. Our organization is dedicated to providing marginalized communities with access to quality education, healthcare, and economic opportunities, thereby empowering them to build better futures for themselves and their children.

At Fowep Nepal, we believe that education is not just a privilege but a fundamental human right. Through our programs and advocacy efforts, we seek to ensure that every child has the opportunity to receive a quality education, regardless of their socio-economic background. We also prioritize the advancement of women's rights, recognizing the pivotal role that women play in building strong and resilient communities.

Our work extends beyond the classroom, encompassing a wide range of initiatives aimed at addressing the root causes of poverty and inequality. From microfinance programs to vocational training workshops, we are committed to equipping individuals with the skills and resources they need to lift themselves out of poverty and build better lives for themselves and their families.

This digital platform serves as a conduit for sharing knowledge, insights, and stories of change. Through My Website, I invite you to join me on a journey of discovery and inspiration as we work towards a more just and compassionate world.

Thank you for taking the time to learn more about my work. Should you wish to connect or learn more about Fowep Nepal's initiatives, I welcome your correspondence with open arms.

With warm regards,

Krishna Chandra Acharya
Chairman, Fowep Nepal
