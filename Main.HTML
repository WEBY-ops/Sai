My Portfolio


A Comprehensive Compilation of My Works and Contributions

Preserving our society requires a concerted effort to addressand mitigate violence in all its forms. Violence not only inflicts physical harm but also erodes the fabric of communities, disrupts social cohesion, and undermines trust among individuals. To combat violence effectively, we must adopt multifaceted approaches that address its root causes while simultaneously promoting peace, understanding, and empathy.I and our team members have done alot of works to to stop violence so everyone can live in peace.


In a series of discussions held with both the SAARC Apex Body and NACG (North American Cooperation Group), the critical issue of child marriage emerged as a focal point. Through these dialogues, we engaged in an in-depth examination of the multifaceted challenges posed by this pervasive practice, seeking collaborative avenues to combat it effectively. Our deliberations centered on the imperative of cross-border cooperation and knowledge exchange to address the underlying causes of child marriage and devise preventive strategies. Recognizing the urgency of the matter, we underscored the importance of joint advocacy initiatives aimed at raising awareness about the adverse consequences of child marriage on the well-being of young individuals and communities. By harnessing the combined resources and expertise of both organizations, we explored innovative approaches to empower vulnerable populations, enhance access to education, and bolster legal frameworks for the protection of children against early and forced marriages. These discussions mark a significant milestone in our shared commitment to eradicating child marriage and fostering a safer, more equitable future for children worldwide.

During a public address, I spoke passionately about the enduring importance of women's rights, recognizing its timeless relevance in societies across the globe. Emphasizing key principles such as equal access to education, healthcare, economic opportunities, and freedom from discrimination and violence, I underscored the necessity of challenging deeply rooted norms that perpetuate gender inequality. Encouraging active participation in advocacy efforts, I urged individuals to join the collective movement towards a more equitable and inclusive society. By fostering a culture of respect and solidarity, we can pave the way for a future where every woman and girl can thrive, unencumbered by limitations on their rights and opportunities.

In a team meeting, we engaged in a dialogue about safeguarding children's rights, recognizing the urgent need to protect the well-being and dignity of every child. Our discussion encompassed various issues such as child labor, access to education, and child protection. We emphasized the importance of raising awareness about the detrimental effects of child labor, advocating for inclusive education opportunities, and creating safe spaces for children to thrive free from violence and abuse. Additionally, we highlighted the significance of empowering children to participate in decision-making processes and ensuring that their voices are heard. Through collaborative efforts and a commitment to prioritizing children's rights, we strive to create a world where every child can flourish and reach their full potential.
